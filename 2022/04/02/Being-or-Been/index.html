Being or Been
YuTeng Lin Trainee
  2022-04-02 15:34:00 2022-04-02 15:34  
  • English 
 
  • English 
 

Being 是 BE 动词的现在分词，而 Been 是 BE 动词的过去分词。

been 一定用在 have (及其他 have 形式，如 has, had, will have 等) 之后，而 being 绝不会用在 have 之后。 Being 是用在 BE 动词之后 (及其他 be 形式，如 are, were, is, was 等)

Example:

  • She is being very clever today. (Correct) 她今天表现的很聪明
  • She has being very clever today. (Incorrect)
  • They have been busy. (Correct) 他们一直很忙
  • They are been busy. (Incorrect)
  • Sophia has being working at that company. (Incorrect) － being 不能在 have 或 has 后。

Being 可用作名词

Example:

  • A human being (人，人类)
  • A strange being stepped out of the spaceship. (一个奇怪的人走出了太空船)

Being 也可用作动名词。

Example:

  • Do you like being so careless? (你喜欢这么粗心吗？)
  • The accident was caused by his being so clumsy. (因为他的不熟练才导致了这起事故)
  • I live in terror of not being misunderstood. (我活在不被误解的恐惧中)。出自英国大文豪王尔德 (Oscar Wilde)

一般而言，现在分词和过去分词都可以当形容词来修饰名词，但 being 和 been 不行

Example:

  • Broken vase 破碎的花瓶 (Correct)
  • Deleted file 删除的文件 (Correct)
  • Jogging girl 跑步的少女 (正)
  • The been car (Incorrect,meaningless)
  • The being flower (Incorrect,meaningless)
